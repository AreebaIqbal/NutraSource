About Us

Welcome to NutraSource

NutraSource international pakistan, is motivated by helping people be as healthy as possible by accurately filling their prescriptions and offering informed advice. Our mission is safeguarding a patient's safety through improving medicines use and reducing medications error.

NutraSource is willing to use their knowledge, experience, and skills to the best of their ability to assure optimal outcomes for their patients.

Oath Of NutraSource

We Promise

To consider the welfare of humanity and relief of suffering our primary concerns.

We Promise

To respect and protect all personal and health information entrusted to us.

We Promise

To embrace and advocate changes that improve patient care.

We Promise

To follow the system which we consider best for Pharmaceutical care.

We Promise

To treat all those we serve equally, fairly and with respect, regardless of anything.

We Promise

To support the advancement of knowledge and standards of practice

We Promise

To endeavor to discover and manufacture drugs of quality to alleviate sufferings of humanity

We Promise

To accept the lifelong obligation to improve our professional knowledge and competence.
